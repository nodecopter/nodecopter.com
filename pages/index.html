What?

NodeCopter.js was a full day event where 60 developers got together into 20 teams of 3.

Each team received one Parrot AR Drone 2.0 and spend the day programming and playing with it. At the end of the day, each team got to present their work to the other attendees.

Want to organize an event?

If you want to do a NodeCopter event in your city (no matter if it's accompanying any conference or not) there is only one requirement: you need to have attended another NodeCopter event before.

The NodeCopter team is here for you to help with budget, logistics, getting the devices and promotion. Feel free to reach out to NodeCopter team on Twitter.
