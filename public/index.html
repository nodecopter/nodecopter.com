Flying robots & node.js

A full day of programming flying robots with
node.js, started in Berlin on October 5 2012.

If you missed the first nodecopter, check out Nodecopter Dublin which Paul Campbell is organizing on October 20th. Most of the original @nodecopter crew will also be there, so you you should sign up now!

We are also planning additional events in the future, so get notified:

What?

NodeCopter.js was a full day event where 60 developers got together into 20 teams of 3.

Each team received one Parrot AR Drone 2.0 and spend the day programming and playing with it. At the end of the day, each team got to present their work to the other attendees.
