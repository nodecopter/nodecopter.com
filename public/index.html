Friday, October 5 2012:

A full day of programming flying robots with
node.js in Berlin (the day before JSConf.eu)!

Maximum of 35-50 people and ~20 EUR ticket price, so get notified when the tickets become available:

What?

NodeCopter.js is a full day event where 30-50 developers will get together in teams of 3.

Each team will receive one Parrot AR Drone 2.0 and spend the day programming and playing with it. At the end of the day, each team will get to present their work to the other attendees.

Why?

Flying robots and node.js!

No seriously, besides having a lot of fun, this event will be great opportunity to explore overlapping fields like Computer Vision, soak up practical knowledge about things like PID Controllers, the Kalman filter and many other fasicinating topics.

You'll be suprised about the opportunties to apply these techniques into your regular web stuff.
